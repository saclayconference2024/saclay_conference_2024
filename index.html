Paris-Saclay Conference on Trade and Environment 2024

The Paris-Saclay Conference on Trade and Environment 2024 will be held on May 13th and 14th, 2024, at Agro Paris-Saclay Campus (Palaiseau, France). The conference is organized by the Paris-Saclay Applied Economics (PSAE) research unit at INRAE and AgroParisTech.

The conference welcomes both advanced and junior researchers in the fields of trade and environmental studies. It aims to provide insightful and high-level academic presentation on the following topics: trade and natural resources, trade and environmental policies, international environmental and trade agreements, green supply chains, etc.

Important Dates

  • Submission deadline: February 15th, 2024
    Please send your (full) paper (in pdf) at: saclay-conference2024@inrae.fr

  • Notification of Acceptance: March 15th, 2024

  • Conference Dates: May 13th and 14th, 2024
