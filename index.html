Call for Papers

We are pleased to announce the Call for Papers for the Paris-Saclay Conference on Trade and Environment 2024, to be held on May 13th and 14th, 2024, at Agro Paris-Saclay Campus. The conference is organized by the research unit Paris-Saclay Applied Economics (PSAE) at INRAE and AgroParisTech.

Thanks to our sponsors, there are no participation fees. Participants will have to cover hotel and travel expenses themselves.

How to submit?

Please send your full paper (in pdf) at: saclay-conference2024@inrae.fr before February 15th, 2024.

Paris-Saclay Conference on Trade and Environment 2024

The conference welcomes both advanced and junior researchers in the fields of trade and environmental studies. It aims to provide insightful and high-level academic presentation on the following topics: trade and natural resources, trade and environmental policies, international environmental and trade agreements, green supply chains, etc.

Important Dates

  • Submission deadline: February 15th, 2024
  • Notification of Acceptance: March 15th, 2024

  • Conference Dates: May 13th and 14th, 2024
