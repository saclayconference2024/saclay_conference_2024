Paris-Saclay Applied Economics (PSAE)

On 1 January 2022, the Paris-Saclay Applied Economics (PSAE) unit was established due to the merger between the ALISS and Public Economics units. The unit is jointly operated by INRAE and AgroParisTech and is headed by Stéphan Marette, with assistance from Basak Bayramoglu and Guy Meunier. In July 2022, PSAE relocated to the new AgroParisTech-INRAE campus at the Université Paris-Saclay. PSAE comprises approximately 50 permanent members from AgroParisTech and INRAE. On average, it hosts 40 students and trainees annually. PSAE is structured around three themes:
  • Food and sustainable behavior
  • Agricultural markets and agri-food chains
  • Sustainable production systems
    • The PSAE’s research aims to evaluate the economic efficiency of public policies in the three thematic areas mentioned above, using theoretical analyses and quantitative methods such as econometrics, experimental economics, and market equilibrium modelling. The economic impacts are quantified through the creation of several databases including the Odalim platform.

    Getting to the Agro Paris-Saclay Campus

    By public transport from Paris

    Take the RER B (-> Saint-Rémy-lès-Chevreuse line) OR RER C (-> Massy-Palaiseau line), to the “Massy-Palaiseau” station, and then take one of the following buses:
    • Bus 91.06 (-> Saint‐Quentin‐en-Yvelines or Christ de Saclay), to the “Palaiseau – Campus” stop.
    • Bus 91.10 (-> Saint‐Quentin – Gare Routière Des Prés bus station), to the “Palaiseau – Campus” stop.

    Or, take the RER B (-> Saint-Rémy-lès-Chevreuse line), to the “Le Guichet” station, and then either:
    • Take the Bus 91.08 toward “Fresnel Vauve/Polytechnique Vauve”, to the “Palaiseau – Campus” stop.
    • Walk from the exit at the rear of the train, located at 3 rue du Pont de Pierre, that leads to the stairs (Passage du Buisson) toward the Plateau de Saclay (a 230-foot difference in elevation), continue straight ahead until the edge of the forest and take the path and the footbridge located in the La Troche forest. When you reach the Plateau de Saclay in the direction of our campus, continue straight ahead.

    By driving from Paris

    • Via N118 (national highway): Porte de Saint Cloud or Pont de Sèvres (either option works), take Exit 8 “Jouy-en-Josas/Saclay”. At the traffic circle, take the first exit toward D36 (departmental road), continue toward “Palaiseau”, and turn right onto Boulevard O, then take Boulevard Gaspard Monge/Madeleine Pelletier. Turn left toward the AgroParisTech parking lot entrance
    • From Porte d’Orléans or Porte d’Italie, take the A6A (or A6B) highway toward Lyon, and continue onto the A10 toward “Nantes/Bordeaux”. Exit left toward “D444/Versailles/Igny/Bièvres”, continue onto D36, and turn left on D128 toward “EDF LAB/Mines Telecom/AgroParisTech”. Turn right on Rue Gaspard Monge, then take Cours Pierre Vasseur.
