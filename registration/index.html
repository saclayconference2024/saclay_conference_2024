Conference Themes

The conference welcomes both advanced and junior researchers in the fields of trade and environmental studies. We encourage submissions that address, but are not limited to, the following topics: trade and natural resources, trade and environmental policies, international environmental and trade agreements, green supply chains, etc.

Agenda
